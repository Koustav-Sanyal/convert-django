About Industrial Trips

The rapid changes that the world is currently going through,coupled with changes in engineering education in this era of 21st century;an opportunity to witness and get hands on experience of the processes and functions of a manufacturing industry or a power generating station is a blessing much sought after especially by the undergraduates as a penultimate before they actually kick-start their career as a young,bright practising engineers.We at ISTE give our best effort for regularly organizing such Industrial visits and are thankful to Damodar Valley Corporation-DTPS,Alloy Steel Plant(Under SAIL),Durgapur,Birla Cement Corporation,Durgapur for being reliable partners in sharing a common vision for uplifting the standards of technical education in India by pursuing our endeavour.
